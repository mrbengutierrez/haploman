After the Restoration

The restoration could not have been done without the help of my wife Clair. Without her the boat would still be an ugly duckling. She single handedly installed all of the new carpet and carpet pad on the boat.

All in all we added the following items:

  • A 100 Watt Solar Panel
  • Two Breaker Boxes
  • 30 Shore Outlet
  • Two New Fuel Pumps
  • Two New Fuel Filters
  • Carpet and Carpet Pad
  • Shower Curtains
  • 750 Watt Bilge Heater
  • 8 Kilowatt Diesel Heater
  • Ducting to Heat Multiple Rooms
  • 27 Gallon Diesel Tank
  • Water Pump

Besides adding upgrades and replacing parts, we also did a fair amount of cleaning. We cleaned every surface of the boat multiple times. In addition, we made sure that that no fungi was among us on the boat. We treated all the surfaces in the bilge with mold cleaner.
